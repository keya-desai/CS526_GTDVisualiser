The Global Terrorism Dataset (GTD) is an open-source database maintained by the National Consortium for the Study of Terrorism and Responses to Terrorism (START) at the University of Maryland. This dataset recorded information on 181,693 terrorist attacks all over the world from 1970 to 2017. This is our attempt at understanding the trends in terrorism through data interaction and visualisation.
